History

  • As mentioned on the main page, the Witcher series is based off of the series of fantasy novels under the same name, by Andrzej Sapkowskili

  • As with the prior two games, the Witcher 3 is set in a world known as The Continent. Just like many other fantasy worlds, The Witcher universe is inhabited by Humans, Elves, Dwarves, dragons and many other creatures.

  • The Witcher becomes exceedingly complicated due to extra dimensions and the like.

Geralt

  • Wondering who that dashing white haired dude is? That's Geralt! He's our main protagonist, and who you will be commandeering on your journey

  • Geralt is a Witcher from the school of the wolves. A Witcher is a rare breed of genetically mutated monster killers. Witchers live and breathe to kill monsters for coin. Witchers' are born human, but undergo intense training and mutations that most do not survive.
